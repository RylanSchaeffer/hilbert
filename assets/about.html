Why Hilbert?

We chose the name Hilbert for two reasons. First, the name is an homage to David Hilbert, one of the greatest mathematicians with sadly too little household name recognition. Second, our mission takes inspiration from Hilbert's Paradox of the Grand Hotel, which teaches us the parable that even when no housing appears to be available, with a little cleverness, we can always make room.
